Free Pickup & Delivery


What could be easier than having your dry cleaning delivered for you? Never having to rush home to pick it up, not worrying if you get there before the cleaners close! That's what! Rest assured, it will be there waiting for you when you get home!

Best part... it’s absolutely FREE with the same prices as if you took them into our store.
Here's how it works
  1. We provide you with two Kona Cleaners Garment bags with your account information on it.
  2. We set up your scheduled delivery days, either Monday/Thursday, or Tuesday/Friday, (Depending on where you live or work.)
  3. We come by your home or office on the scheduled days to pick up, and return the clean garments from the previous pick up. If you do not have anything to send in that day, no worries, we'll come back again on the next scheduled day. We are in your neighborhood anyways.
  4. We automatically bill your credit card on file, so you dont need to be home to pay for services!
